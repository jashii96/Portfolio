  • Project date: July, 2018

Online Examination Portal is a software application which allows institute or college to arrange, conduct & manage any objective examination via online. This project is developed using ASP.NET & MS ACCESS database as backend of the project.

The wbesite to conduct online examination is 'Online Examination POrtal'. This website provides facility to institutes to conduct online exams by providing a unique id to each. The institute provides questions along with positive & negative marks. institute also enter the list of eligible students. All the information entered can be later edited by the institute.

The online exam system design for education institutes & colleges.

Hold all operation & generate reports to student, teachers & administrator. Support multiple questions.

Allow the student to pro choice the answer & to see his marks. Verify a security, authority & safety.
